Our Services

Four trades. End-to-end care.

Every service is delivered in-house by trained, certified marine technicians from our base at Coomera Marina.

01 — Mechanical

Engine Maintenance & Repairs

Whether you run a single 50 hp outboard or twin 1,200 hp inboards, our engineers keep your power plant smooth, fuel-efficient and reliable. We diagnose, service, rebuild and repower across all major brands.

  • Scheduled servicing — 100 hr, 250 hr, annual
  • Diagnostics, fault-find and warranty work
  • Top-end and full-engine rebuilds
  • Repowers — outboard, sterndrive and shaft
  • Fuel system, cooling, exhaust and gearbox
  • Pre-purchase mechanical inspections

Brands serviced: Mercury, Yamaha, Suzuki, Honda, Volvo Penta, Cummins, Yanmar, MAN, Caterpillar, MTU.

02 — Electrical

Marine Electrical & Electronics

From shore-power and house-bank installs to full nav and comms refits, our licensed marine electricians design and install systems that hold up to salt, vibration and survey.

  • DC & AC systems — 12V, 24V, 240V, three-phase
  • Lithium, AGM and gel battery banks
  • Solar, inverter and shore-power integration
  • Switchboards, distribution and metering
  • Navigation, AIS, radar, autopilot and comms
  • Lighting, entertainment and onboard automation

Compliance: AS/NZS 3004.2, ABYC, NMEA 2000 and AMSA standards.

03 — Hull & Body

Structural & Hull Repairs

From a small gel chip to a major structural rebuild, our shipwrights and composite specialists work to survey-grade tolerances. We blend, fair and finish to a level you'd be proud to show off at the yacht club.

  • Fibreglass, GRP and composite repairs
  • Gelcoat and topcoat work — colour-matched
  • Osmosis treatment and full anti-fouling
  • Structural keel, rudder and stringer repair
  • Deck recore, fairing and non-skid
  • Insurance-approved collision and damage repair

Materials: GRP/FRP, carbon, Kevlar, marine-grade timber, Coosa, Divinycell.

04 — Project Management

Project Management & Refits

For owners who want one accountable contact across an entire scope of work — refit, restoration, new commissioning or insurance rebuild. We hold the schedule, the budget, the trades and the quality, and report to you weekly.

  • Full scope-of-work and budget development
  • Trade coordination and supplier management
  • Slipway, lift and bay scheduling at Coomera Marina
  • Weekly photo & cost progress reports
  • Sea trial, commissioning and handover packs
  • Aftercare warranty and ongoing service plan

Typical projects: mid-life refits, repowers, paint & gel projects, electronics refresh, new-build commissioning, insurance damage rebuilds.
