Our Home Port

Operating from Coomera Marina.

Coomera Marina is the largest dry-berth and refit facility on the Gold Coast — and it's where we work, every day. That gives us direct access to slipways, hardstand, travel lifts, undercover bays and a network of specialist trades all on one site.

For owners in Brisbane, the Broadwater or up the Coomera River, that means one gate, one team, one bill — and your boat back in the water on schedule.

  • Up to 70-tonne travel lift access
  • Undercover and air-conditioned refit bays
  • Direct waterway access — no road transport needed
  • Secure 24/7 berthing during works
