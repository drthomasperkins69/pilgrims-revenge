Who We Are

Working boats. Working owners.

Pilgrims Revenge was founded by working marine tradesmen who got tired of seeing owners shuffled between yards, blamed by sub-contractors, and handed bills they didn't understand. We do it differently.

Every job has a single project lead. Every quote is itemised. Every photo, invoice and warranty card lives in one shared file. And every vessel — tender, cruiser or superyacht — gets the same standard of work.

From quick-turn services up the Coomera River to multi-month refits at the marina, we keep the same simple promise: we treat your boat like we'd treat ours.

Coomera Marina

The biggest refit yard on the Gold Coast.

Coomera Marina is the largest dry-storage and repair facility in the southern hemisphere — over 600 hardstand bays, slipways, travel-lifts and a full network of marine trades all on a single site.

We're integrated into the marina, which means we don't sub-contract the haul-out, the bay or the slipway — we book it through our standing arrangement and run the job ourselves. That cuts handover time, removes finger-pointing and keeps your boat in one set of hands.

  • Direct waterway access from Brisbane & Broadwater
  • Travel lift up to 70 tonnes
  • Dedicated paint, antifoul and refit bays
  • Full chandlery and parts on site
  • Secure 24/7 berthing during works
  • Owners welcome on-site any working day
